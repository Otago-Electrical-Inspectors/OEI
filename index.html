Warrant of Electrical Fitness

Oe inspectors can take the hassle out of your next Warrant of Electrical Fitness (WOEF) for your caravan, motorhome or food truck. Our inspectors will come to where the vehicle is located for your convenience.

WHY IS AN WOEF IMPORTANT?

Holiday Park operators will require your caravan or motorhome to have a current WOEF before allowing you to connect on a powered site. Also local authorities will require a current WOEF for a food truck before connecting into a public power point.

It is a requirement under the Electricity Act 1992 that any connectable installation be certified before being connected to a power supply.


Caravan, motorhomes and food trucks are small confined spaces, often damp and are constantly on the move therefore they are of higher risk for deterioration and accidental damage. Regular checks are important to ensure the safety of people using these spaces.

WHERE DO I GET AN WOEF INSPECTION?

Oe inspectors will perform your inspection on site at your home or storage location. Any matters that require rectifying will be notified at the time of testing. If your inspection does not meet the regulations the first time, there will be a small charge for a revisit.
